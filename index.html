About Me

My primary research interest is algorithms. Recently, I have been focusing on the intersection of machine learning and discrete optimization, particularly the use of graph neural networks both as a direct method for solving combinatorial optimization problems and as a way to enhance classical approaches. I'm also interested in the theory of graph neural networks, particularly their relationship to dynamic programming, iterative algorithms, and fixed-point methods on combinatorial structures.

Research Interests

  • Graph Neural Networks
  • Graph Theory
  • Combinatorial Optimization
